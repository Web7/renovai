Inside Sales Manager

  • Full Time
  • Tel-Aviv
  • Apply here

Description

renovai is rapidly growing and looking for an experienced, motivated, innovative, and data-driven Inside Sales Manager to structure the sales process and generate new leads.
In this role, the SDM will have a significant impact in shaping renovai's growth strategy and work closely with our CEO to later hire and lead the sales team.

Responsibilities:

  • Create and drive GTM strategy
  • Build clients' profiles and set criteria for initiating contact
  • Establish account-based marketing methodology
  • Creating an out-bound automatic process
  • Proactively own and manage the process of contacting new leads
  • Handling lead-generation process, including managing Upwork teams
  • Creating sequences and script emails
  • Communicate with clients in a customized and scalable manner
  • Plan campaigns and work closely with our marketing and content managers
  • Develop thought leadership initiatives that strengthen brand positioning

Requirements:

  • Minimum 3 years of experience in a B2B SaaS or Service customer-facing position
  • Minimum 1 of year cold outbound experience
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Strong knowledge of market analytics and research tools
  • Proven track record of planning, executing and measuring a complete sales strategy from A to Z.
  • Excellent communication skills and methodical thinking process
  • Strong operational capabilities - comfortable with managing multiple projects for multiple different audiences/stakeholders

  • eCommerce experience - advantage
  • Basic tech understanding - must
